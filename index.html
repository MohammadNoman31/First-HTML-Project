More About Invertis:

Invertis University is a private university located in Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh, India. It is situated on Bareilly-Lucknow NH-24, 290 km from the national capital Delhi and 240 km from the state capital Lucknow. The Chancellor of the university is Umesh Gautam and the Vice-Chancellor is Y. D. S. Arya.
